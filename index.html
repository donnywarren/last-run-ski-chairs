ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Last Run Ski Chairs are uniquely handcrafted by Carson Parco, a 17-year old ski racer (with an adult mentor), who is competing at the FIS or Federation Internationale de Ski level. He aspires to attend college and ski at the World Cup level and ultimately ski at the Olympic Games. He could not find a job at age 15 and started this entrepreneural endeavor to earn funds to puchase ski racing equipment and gear, as well as be able to attend camps and races.
